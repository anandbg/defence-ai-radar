About Defence-AI Radar

Defence-AI Radar consolidates the latest publicly-known advancements in artificial intelligence across the defence sector — governments, agencies, prime contractors and startups — into one continuously-updated picture. Every entry is drawn entirely from the public domain and links back to its original source.

How it stays current

An automated pipeline runs every day: it scans official government feeds, agency announcements and the defence press for new developments and refreshes the timeline automatically. The organisation dossiers are synthesised from public sources and reviewed regularly.

A note on completeness

This is open-source intelligence, so it will inevitably miss things — the public record is always partial. If you spot something missing, outdated or incorrect, please email Anand at anandbg@gmail.com with the source and it will be updated.

Public-domain only

Nothing here is classified or non-public. Every claim is sourced and linked back to its origin.
